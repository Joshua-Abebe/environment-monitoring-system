"The Environmental Monitoring Platform is designed as a modular, event-driven system where every event is traceable,
every component is observable, and every service has a single responsibility."

"Every event tells a story. Every service contributes one chapter. Observability tells the whole story."


Term	                                    Meaning
Event	                     The atomic unit of information flowing through the platform.
Session	                     A single execution lifecycle of the platform.
Observability	             The ability to understand the internal state through metrics, traces, logs, and health.
Digital Twin	             A live representation of the running platform.
Event Trace	                 The complete lifecycle of one event identified by its UUID.
Pipeline	                 The ordered path an event follows through the system.
Persistence	                 Long-term storage of events in one or more databases.
Platform	                 The complete ecosystem of services, infrastructure, and interfaces.
